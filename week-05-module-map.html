🎯 MODULE OBJECTIVES
1
Evaluate Charlemagne's reign as an example of Great-Man history: conquest, coronation, and a deliberate revival of learning.
2
Explain how the fragmentation of the Carolingian empire and renewed outside raiding produced feudalism as a practical response to the collapse of centralized authority.
3
Describe the role of monasteries as centers of education, charity, economic productivity, and political power in medieval Western Europe.
