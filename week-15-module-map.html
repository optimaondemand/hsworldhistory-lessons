🎯 MODULE OBJECTIVES
1
Trace the forerunners, causes, and spread of the Protestant Reformation through Luther, Zwingli, Calvin, and the radical reformers.
2
Explain the English Reformation under Henry VIII and evaluate the fates of Thomas More and Thomas Cranmer against the Classical Republican standard of internal order.
3
Describe the Catholic Church's Counter-Reformation response through the Council of Trent and new religious orders.
