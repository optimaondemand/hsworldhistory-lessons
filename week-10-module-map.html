Module 6 · The Mongol Storm and the Crisis of the Late Middle Ages

🎯 MODULE OBJECTIVES
1
Trace the Mongol conquests across Eurasia and evaluate why they succeeded in China, the Middle East, and Russia, but failed against Japan.
2
Compare Japanese and Western European feudalism, and describe Japan's cultural and economic relationship to China and Korea.
3
Explain how urbanization and universities revived Western intellectual and economic life, even as famine, plague, schism, and war brought the High Middle Ages to a violent close.
